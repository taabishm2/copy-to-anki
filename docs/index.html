FAQ

Is Copy to Anki free?

Manual card creation is free. If you enable auto-generating questions, you’ll need an OpenAI API key and an OpenAI account with credits/billing enabled.

What data does it store?

Copy to Anki stores your settings (like deck/model choices) and any queued notes locally in your browser. To add cards it talks to AnkiConnect on your machine (localhost). If you enable AI question generation, the selected text is sent to OpenAI.

Will it work on any website?

It works on most pages; complex sites may require manual tweaks.
